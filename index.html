LIFESKILLS 4 CHILDREN is an innovative company built to teach your children skills they don't learn in school. With curriculum pressures, other skills, such as essential lifeskills, are often neglected. We at LIFESKILLS 4 CHILDREN believe these are just as important and time needs to be spent to teach them. Our sessions are aimed on teaching children how to UNDERSTAND, PROBLEM SOLVE and THINK about all the challenges life throws their way, not just the ones in school. So have a look around and get in contact, it's never too late or early to learn!
